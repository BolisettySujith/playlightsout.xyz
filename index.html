What's Lights Out?

Lights Out is a modern reimagining of the classic puzzle game that has been challenging players for decades. Your goal is simple yet deceptively tricky: turn off all the lights on the grid by flipping tiles, where each move toggles the state of a tile and its neighbors, requiring strategic thinking to solve the puzzle.

  • Custom Tile Themes: Personalize your game with vibrant, glowing, or emoji styles.
  • Daily Challenges: Test your skills with new puzzles every day.
  • Adjustable Grid Sizes: Choose your difficulty with varying grid dimensions.
  • Endless Fun: Enjoy brain-teasing excitement that keeps you coming back.
  • For Everyone: Perfect for puzzle enthusiasts and casual gamers alike, offering a fresh experience every time.
